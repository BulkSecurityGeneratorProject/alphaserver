Capacity and DR Planning

Capacity Planning: Planning for future organizational growth and capacity is the cornerstone of a strategic approach to business and IT environments. It is the science and art of estimating the space, hardware, software and infrastructure resources that will be needed over some future period of time. We provide advisory services which will weigh your current environment review, along with understanding the IT and business growth expectations, to deliver sound recommendations for optimal results.

Disaster Recovery: Contingency planning is the best form of infrastructure risk mitigation. Every business or organization can experience a serious incident that can hinder ongoing normal operations. Our IT professionals provide advisory services, in conjunction with software licensing of multiple environments, to insure that you are covered for any eventuality and can be sure of high- availability with minimum downtime.
